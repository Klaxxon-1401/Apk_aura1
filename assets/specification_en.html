Instruction Manual

This product includes an ir blaster, a mobile app, a keychain, etc. (For more detailed instructions, please refer to the "Profile" section of the app).

Click the link to watch the operation video. http://app.ELKsmart.com

 

1. Download the APP

iOS phones : Scan the QR code or search for "Aura IR" in the App Store.

Android phones  :   Scan the QR code or search for "Aura IR" the Google Play. Scan the QR code or search for " Aura IR " in the Google Play.

 

2. Insert the IR Blaster

Open the downloaded APP, remove the ir blaster from the silicone case, insert it into the phone's charging port, and ensure the end with the ir blaster is close to and aimed at the appliance.

iOS Phone  :   After the blaster is properly inserted, wait for 3-5 seconds for a pop-up window to appear, then select the corresponding ir blaster.

Before using an iOS phone to control home appliances, please make sure to turn off the following settings (which are not enabled by default).

1) Turn off Mono Audio (Settings > Accessibility > Audio&Visual > Mono Audio)

2) Turn off Equalizer (Settings > Music > EQ)

Please note: If you cannot find the "Music" option, please re-download the official "Music" app from the App Store.

3) For iPhone 15+ USB-C interface phones, you also need to turn off (Settings > Sounds& Haptics > Headphone Safety > USB Audio Accessories > Connected To HeadphonesHeadp)

Android Phones  : A pop-up window should display within 1 second after the blaster is correctly inserted, if not, please manually open the OTG function in the system phone settings.

1.It has been confirmed that some models of vivo, OPPO, Xiaomi, OnePlus, and Realme must manually turn on the OTG function.

 

3.Match the Appliance     

1) Open the APP, click on the " "button or the" "button at the top left; go to the "Remote Type" page to select the appliance type, and then select the appliance brand.

2)Follow the prompts to complete the appliance matching, and select the most appropriate remote control code based on the matching. Each brand may have multiple remote control codes (for example, 3/8 indicates that the brand has 8 sets of remote control codes, and you are trying the 3rd set), follow the operation prompts to press the button one by one (the green light of the ir blaster flashing proves that the remote control code has been sent), if the appliance responds correctly, it means the button has been matched, then proceed to the next button matching until the appliance matching is completed.

*Automatic Matching Feature:  If the air conditioner cannot connect with "Automatic Matching," you can try "Manual Matching."

*IR Copying Function:  Select "IR Copying" in the appliance type, and follow the steps to learn the recording.

This function allows you to copy any button from your physical infrared remote control to the app for use.

*The function is only supported by some product models, if the ir blaster is inserted and the app does not have it, it means it is not supported.

 

4. Save the Remote Control

After a successful match, the APP will automatically save the remote control. You can modify the remote control name or delete the remote control by swiping left or long-pressing the remote control name.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

1.What if the phone does not recognize the ir blaster after downloading and installing the APP?  

1）Android phones, please confirm again whether OTG is turned on.

2）iOS phones, please select the corresponding product and try again after turning off Bluetooth.

3）If there is a thick phone case, please try again after removing the phone case.

2. The phone can recognize the ir blaster, but it cannot search for the brand model, or the remote control function is incomplete. What could be the reason?

1）The ir blaster can only control infrared appliances, and most televisions with voice control on the remote control are currently not supported (televisions with voice control on the remote control are generally Bluetooth remote controls).

2）If you cannot find the corresponding brand, you can search for it or try the brand's English name.

3）If some buttons of the remote control are incorrect, you can go back to the matching process and try the next numbered remote control code.

 

Precautions

1. Android and iOS are not universal; the Android version Type-C (black plug core) cannot be used interchangeably with the Apple version iPhone 15+ USB-C interface (orange plug core).

2. The APP requires authorization for some permissions, and we ensure that the relevant functions and information will not be misused.

Not all appliances can be controlled with our APP code library. If you encounter an unsupported appliance model during use, you can provide feedback by email, and we will do our best to add and supplement it later late. （Alice@ELKsmart.com)
